EXPLANATIONS OF THE VIOLATIONS



1)SINGLE RESPONSIBILITY PRINCIPLE -> LOCATED IN ItemController.cs -> GetAll()
  
  Why is it a violation? 
     SRP says that a class/method must have only one motive for change,but in the code,SRP has a lot of responsibilites.

   Fixes I applied: I reduced the logic outside the controller and used ILogger instead of Console.WriteLine.



2)SINGLE RESPONSIBILITY PRINCIPLE ->LOCATED IN ItemController.cs -> GetById()

  Why is it a violation?
     The controller should not bear logging low-level.

  Fixes I applied: I made a standard logging.


3)OPEN/CLOSE PRINCIPLE -> LOCATED IN itemController.cs ->GetAll()

  Why is it a violation?
      API's structure cannot be reused,it has to be changed directly in the controller.

  Fixes I applied: simple structure.
